# Privacy Policy for Calorie Tracker

**Last Updated: February 26, 2026**

Calorie Tracker ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, Calorie Tracker.

## 1. Information We Collect

Calorie Tracker is designed to be a local-first application. We prioritize your privacy by keeping your data on your device.

### a. Personal Data
When you onboarding the app, you may provide information such as:
- Name
- Age
- Gender
- Height
- Weight
- Activity Level
- Nutritional Goals

### b. Usage Data
The app tracks your food entries, calorie intake, and macronutrient consumption.

**Important:** All of the data mentioned above is stored locally on your device using `shared_preferences`. We do not transmit this data to any external servers or third-party services.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely within the app to:
- Calculate your BMR (Basal Metabolic Rate) and TDEE (Total Daily Energy Expenditure).
- Set daily calorie and macronutrient targets.
- Track your progress toward your nutritional goals.
- Provide a personalized dashboard and meal log.

## 3. Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your mobile device. We do not have access to your personal information or usage data. 

- **Local Storage:** Your data remains on your device and is deleted if you uninstall the application (unless backed up by your operating system's standard backup mechanisms).
- **Security:** Since we do not collect or transmit your data, the security of your information depends on the security of your mobile device.

## 4. Third-Party Services

Calorie Tracker does not share your data with any third-party services. The app does not contain analytics or advertising frameworks that track your behavior across other apps or websites.

## 5. Children's Privacy

Calorie Tracker does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us so we can take steps to ensure it is not stored or accessed.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
